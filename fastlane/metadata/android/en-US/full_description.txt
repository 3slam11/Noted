Noted is your all-in-one entertainment tracker for movies, TV series, books, and games. Organize your entertainment journey with To-Do and Finished lists, and experience our unique monthly rollover that moves completed items to history while letting you carry over unfinished ones. 

Key Features:
• Track entertainment across Movies, TV Series, Books, and Games
• Smart monthly rollover with history archiving
• Search new titles using RAWG, TMDB, and Google Books APIs
• Detailed item information with descriptions, ratings, and images
• Consumption statistics and history tracking
• Custom API key support
• Local backup/restore
• Dynamic theming (auto-monthly or manual)
• Multi-language localization support

Never lose track of your entertainment adventures again. Manage your watchlist, discover new favorites, and analyze your habits - all in one minimalist designed app.
